the migration

One day, in a very long time, three (3) of the children of Papaga Ashiapim moved to Anlo-Land. They came to Anloga because the people of Anlo invited them to come and help them. So, Eha, ....., ...., left their nine (9) siblings/brothers at Ada and came to Anlo land. When they came, they carry with them their war god to use.
