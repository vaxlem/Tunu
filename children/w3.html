ACTIVITIES

FISHING

Main occupation here is fishing. Different kinds of fishes are here. Some, like crabs have hard skin while others like catfish have flesh.

The source of the stream/creek is a tributary from the VOLTA RIVER joining the sea at Azizanu - Fuveme.(Ada Foah)
People can rear their own fish as well.

RIVER-SPORTS

Water Sports is possible on this stream.

It is both shallow and deep at some ends.

A nice centre in Anlo kingdom. This can generate millions of Cedis for the inhabitants. The water current is good. Tunu Map People can invest in this area.

You can see is, these kinds of fishes. Crabs, lobsters and other shell animals, tilapia, mudfish, red fish, catfish, etc. Some refers to locally as: [AKPA, ADEHE, ADZORVIA, KPOTOKUI, GALA, BLOLO, FWA, KPORLOR, BORLU, ADZA and many more. ]

It is the center of hatching for the sea creatures (fishes) because the water changes from salt to fresh and back to salty following the seasons that each fish kind lays eggs and breeds.

Major occupation for the people is to catch fish for food and to sell. The CREEK (tributary channel) provides a good environment for lots of water living creatures to breed. This is because the area serves as a nursery where some sea fishes as well as the lagoon and the river volta fishes come to hatch their eggs. Above are some kinds of fishes that the people catch.
