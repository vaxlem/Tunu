Tunu.

According to the narratives, the name Tunu is an appellation that is coined by these men. Preku and Lakle Tay over centuries ago.

FULL STORY.

One day in a very long time, a man called Papa Preku went to hunt wild animals in the place. He was a hunter. The animals were disturbing on this hill. So Preku was sent by his partner Lakle to go on the hunting expedition. Upon going, he set his trap by fixing a gun on the pathway where the beasts move on or pass when they are roaming.
