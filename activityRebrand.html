The Rich City

Tunu is a fishing comminity. Together with few towns by the river tributary, they form the Anyanui State. Thus one of the 36 States of ANLO.There is lots of Mangrove here. Bomigo is her Twin City. The city can be divided into two parts, the Wet Swampy area and the dryland on the right.

What?

A home place to be.

As a sovering State among the 36 states of Anlo, Tunu township engages in numerous livelyhood activities. Here you'll see some works that keep them busy each day

Where?

Tunu is at the mouth of the running tributary that connects and feeds the Keta lagoon from the west.See Map

How?

You can reach Tunu township by two (2) means. By road network and Cruisane on the tributary stream that connects the large Keta lagoon on the eastern part and the estuary to the west.
