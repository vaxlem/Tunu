TUNU

According to the narratives, the name TUNU is an appalation that is coined by these men. Preku and Lakle Tay



FULL STORY

One day in a very long time, a man called papa Preku went to hunt for wild animals in tne place. the animals were disturbing. preku was sent by his partner Lakle to go on the hunting expedition. upon going he set his trap by fixing the gun on the path that the beast patronage or pass when they are roaming. After setting is gun he would climb a tree and by means of a rope he will pull the triger of the gun on the ground at the animals. He would then complete the killing by the other riffles and amunitions he had with him on the top of the tree. this bravery amazed all other hunters and the people and they wonder how he did it. therefore they would ask him how he did it.
And proudly kpeku will say *this place is a egdge bof the burrel.when one tries one dies.
This fame naturally resulted in the Famous name TUNU. (at the barrel of a gun.)
